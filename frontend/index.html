reframe your resume
for any role.

Upload your resume, paste a job description. A multi-agent pipeline reads the JD, finds the gaps, and rewrites your bullets to surface the exact words that role asks for. Same fonts, same layout, only the words inside the bullets change.

See how it works
scroll↓
01 extraction
Reads the JD. Pulls every keyword.
The system tokenizes the posting into the exact skills, tools, and seniority signals an ATS would scan for. Each token detaches from the JD page and stages between the two documents.
02 matching
Maps tokens to your bullets.
Each JD requirement is routed to the bullet that already covers it, or flagged as a gap. Covered bullets glow. Missing keywords are queued for the rewrite pass.
03 rewriting
Rewrites the words. Keeps the layout.
Each weak bullet is rewritten in place to surface the JD's vocabulary. Truthful. Never invents numbers or experience. The page itself, fonts, sizes, margins, bullet markers, does not move.
04 delivery
Returns it. Pixel for pixel.
The tailored resume comes back as a .docx or .pdf in the exact same visual format you spent hours getting right. Optionally with a matched cover letter.
// six specialists

One orchestrator. Six specialists.

Each agent does one thing well. The orchestrator runs them in sequence, then a format-preserving writer puts the new words back exactly where the old ones lived.

01 · JD Reader
Reads the JD.
Pulls required skills, seniority, and ATS keywords from the posting.
02 · Parser
Reads your resume.
Identifies sections, bullets, contact info, and the candidate's actual story.
03 · Matcher
Finds the gaps.
Maps what you have to what the JD wants. Covered, weak, or missing.
04 · Rewriter
Rewrites bullets.
Surfaces JD vocabulary in your existing claims, truthfully, never fabricating.
05 · ATS Scorer
Scores it.
Computes keyword coverage and flags terms still missing for ATS systems.
06 · Cover Letter
Drafts the letter.
Writes a matched cover letter using the resume, JD, and the gaps the matcher found.
